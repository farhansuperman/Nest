Frequently Asked Questions

You can submit a support request through our online form. Simply fill out all required fields and our team will respond within 24 hours.

Make sure to provide as much detail as possible about your issue so we can assist you more effectively.

To help us assist you better, please include:

  • A detailed description of your issue
  • Any error messages you've encountered
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Your account or reference number if applicable

This information helps our support team diagnose and resolve your issue faster.

After submitting your request, you'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number. You can use this number to track the status of your request in our support portal.

You can also log into your account dashboard to view all your open and closed tickets.

We prioritize all requests and aim to respond within 24 hours. Complex issues may require additional time, and we'll keep you informed throughout the process.

Urgent matters can be flagged in your request for expedited handling.
